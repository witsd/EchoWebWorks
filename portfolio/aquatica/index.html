Questions

Before you dive in.

I'm a complete beginner — is that really okay?+

It's exactly who we built Aquatica for. Most of our beginners have never swum a length in their life. You'll start in shallow, warm water and progress only when you're ready.

What do I need to bring?+

Just swimwear, a towel, and goggles if you have them. We provide everything else, including floats and training aids.

How big are the classes?+

Never more than four swimmers per coach. Private one-to-one sessions are also available on request.

When do sessions run?+

Seven days a week — mornings, evenings, and weekends — so lessons fit around your work, not the other way round.

Ready to dive in?

Tell us where you are with swimming and we'll match you to the perfect program — usually within a day.

No commitment — your first session is a relaxed assessment in the water.
