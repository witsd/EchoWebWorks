FAQ

Common questions

What services do you offer?+

A comprehensive range including paint correction, ceramic coating, paint protection film, interior detailing, and maintenance packages — each tailored to your vehicle's needs.

How long does the detailing process take?+

A basic interior detail typically takes 3–4 hours; a full paint correction with ceramic coating can take 2–3 days. We confirm an accurate timeframe at booking.

How much does a detailing service cost?+

Pricing depends on vehicle size, condition, and services. Packages start at $149, with comprehensive correction and coating treatments from $1,500+. Contact us for a personalised quote.

Do you offer warranties?+

Yes. Ceramic coatings carry a 2–5 year warranty depending on the product, and paint protection film includes a 10-year warranty against yellowing, cracking, and peeling.

What is ceramic coating?+

A liquid polymer that chemically bonds with your factory paint, protecting against UV, chemical stains, and minor scratches — while creating a hydrophobic, easy-clean surface.

Do I need an appointment?+

Yes — we work by appointment only so every vehicle gets proper time and attention. Book online or call the studio.

What payment methods do you accept?+

All major credit and debit cards, cash, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Financing options are available for larger projects.
